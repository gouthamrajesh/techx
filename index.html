What is TechX 23

TechX '23 is a cutting-edge summit organized by IEEE Computer Society SBC GEC Barton Hill, Thiruvananthapuram in collaboration with IEEE Computer Society Student and Young Professionals Activity Committee MGA Board. This event aims to redefine the boundaries of technological excellence by offering diverse tracks, workshops, competitions, and the opportunity to interact with esteemed tech experts. With a strong focus on fostering innovation and technological growth, TechX '23 offers an exclusive national platform for delegates to showcase their talents and expertise, making it a must-attend summit for all technology enthusiasts.
